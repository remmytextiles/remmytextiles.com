Established in 2019, Remmy Textiles is a third-generation family-based company. We are an emerging manufacturer and exporter of knitted garments with an integrated factory producing over 12 million pcs/year. Our core competency lies in understanding our customers' buying preferences and delivering consistent, high-quality products that meet international compliance standards. With a keen eye on the latest fashion trends, we are committed to providing exceptional products that exceed our customers' expectations.

INFRASTRUCTURE

  • SPINNING MILL

    State-of-the-art spinning mill with 18,000 spindles. Producing 4200 metric tons of cotton yarn annually (10s to 40s counts). Highly automated systems ensure top-quality yarn meeting high standards. Advanced contamination control.

  • KNITTING DIVISION

    Our facility produces single, spandex, double jersey, and fleece grey fabrics. Capacity: 2500 metric tons/year. Ensuring high-quality output, we collaborate with long-standing partners for track designs, feeder stripes, autostripes, and jacquard fabrics.

  • PROCESSING MILL

    3500 tons/year finished fabrics. Turkey-made DMS Dilmenler machinery for dyeing, drying, stentering, and compacting. Vessels 25-2000 kg, high/low-temp capabilities. Datacolor's Match Textile for accurate, speedy recipe calculation. Superior results!

  • PRINTING

    SEDEX approved printing. 20k-30k units/day. Conventional, manual, MHM Automatic (14-16 colors). In-house design team for fast developments. Top-notch quality, efficient production, reliable support. Join us for innovation and creativity.

  • EMBROIDERY

    Barudan's Japanese machines offer precise embroidery. 15k units/day production capacity. Trust us for quality sequence embroidery, stone works, stud work, and embellishments. Elevate your brand now!

WHY US?

  • SOPs

    We follow Kanban-based Progressive Bundle System for JIT and Lean Manufacturing. This visual inventory tool controls production, moves inventory efficiently, and prevents stockpile by restocking only when reserves are low.

  • QUALITY ASSURANCE

    Our well-organized Quality Assurance team conducts continuous online checks from incoming fabric to finished garments, utilizing the latest technologies. This ensures flawless garment quality.

  • TESTING CAPABILITY

    Remmy Textiles ensures top-notch quality with comprehensive testing capabilities, including Dimensional Stability to Washing for both fabric and garments, Fabric Weight (GSM), Spirality, Appearance After Wash (Single, Three, Five Washes), Print Durability (Single, Three, Five Washes), Colour Fastness to Washing and Rubbing, and Button Pull Strength Test.

  • EMPOWERING COMMUNITIES, ENSURING SUSTAINABILITY

    Remmy Textiles actively promotes well-being and sustainability through CSR. Vaccination drive (3000), school & healthcare donations uplift communities. Embracing eco-friendly practices: wind energy, water purification, rainwater harvesting. Committed to employee welfare, strict compliance. A responsible, caring, sustainable partner.
